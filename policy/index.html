Menaz: Game Mode Manager

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 05, 2026

Thank you for using Menaz: Game Mode Manager. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email (except for support), or phone number.

Device & Usage Data

  • Device model and Android version
  • RAM usage statistics (local only)
  • Battery level and charging status (local only)
  • List of installed apps (for game detection - processed locally)
  • Your added "My Games" list (stored locally on your device)

Permissions Requested

  • Do Not Disturb Access — To block notifications during Game Mode
  • Display Over Other Apps — For potential floating controls (future feature)
  • Usage Access — To detect when you launch games (optional auto-activation)
  • Other Permissions — Screen brightness control and keep screen on

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to provide core functionality of the Game Mode Manager:

  • Enable and manage Game Mode features
  • Show real-time RAM and battery statistics
  • Remember your game list and settings
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage (SharedPreferences). We do not upload your data to any external servers.

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are integrated.

4. Third Parties

Menaz does not share your data with any third parties. The app operates completely offline with no internet connection required for core features.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke any granted permissions in Android Settings
  • Clear app data or uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Contact us to request information about your data

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
